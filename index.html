Our story

DaddyChill started in 2020 as four long lost siblings searching for the perfect balance of depression, anxiety, and queerness. DaddyChill Services LLC now sells a selection of sessions and consultations online and over the phone.

We have a philosophy of balance—a balance of old techniques and new ones, of different mental illnesses, and of swaginess and gayness.
